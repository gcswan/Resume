My Story

I built this site in an effort to convince potential employers that I am more than just a resume that crosses your desk. I am many things and I’m here to sell you on that notion.

I am a web developer, a filmmaker, and a lifelong student of both. Frankly, I just enjoy building things and telling stories. As a kid I was obsessed with lego. I would build a city, a bank, or a boat, rebuild it a couple of times, and then play out the different narrative scenarios I had created with tailor made lego figures. As an adult, I still enjoy the same things, I've just replaced my legos with HTML and JavaScript libraries. At heart I’m still the same creative innovator. It’s not out of the ordinary for me to dive right into a project without any previous idea of what I’m doing and try to create something new. That’s how I learn. I try and I fail- I rinse and I repeat.

In the past year alone I have taught myself how to build a static site from scratch. With my Github repository in place, I built a beginners web portfolio using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, a little jQuery, and a lot of help from Stack Overflow. This was my first step into the world of web development. With a desire to further my education in web development, I enrolled in Austin Coding Academy’s full stack nine month bootcamp. Since enrolling in ACA, I have spent most of my time studying Javascript and becoming fluent in technologies such as Node and React. These are just a few of the steps I have taken this year in an effort to become a better developer. So what comes next? My short term goals include- learning my second programming language, building a full stack web application from the ground up, and the obvious one.. getting hired as junior web developer!
